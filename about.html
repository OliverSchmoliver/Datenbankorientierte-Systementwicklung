We are AirplaneFood!

We are a band from Innsbruck, Austria. We play a mix of rock, pop and indie music. Our music is inspired by bands like Kaleo, Artic Monkeys, RHCP, etc.

It all started when our mom's met our dad's, and then there was us. Unfortunatly, we didn't meet until we were in high school, to play in the school band.

What started as a fun hobby became a passion for every single one of us. From playing at the local open mic to sold out stadiums (soon) in under a year

Feel free to come visit us on our social medias or drop by a gig, but most importantly always reach out if you want us to play at yours :)
